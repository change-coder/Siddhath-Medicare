Frequently Asked Questions

What are the different modes of payment available?

You can use any one of the following payment methods:

  1. Credit card of all leading Banks
  2. Debit of all leading banks
  3. Net Banking
  4. UPI Payments
  5. E-Wallets

Are there any hidden charges while making payment?

There are absolutely no hidden charges. You pay only the amount that you see in your order summary page. In some cases, if the courier delivery person asks for any extra charges then kindly contact us.

What are the shipping charges?

  1. When you place any order of less than 500gm then a shipping charge of Rs.50 gets added to your bill.
  2. When you place any order between 501gm to 999gm then a shipping charge of Rs.75 gets added to your bill.
  3. The shipping charges are waived off when you place an order of above Rs.1000.

How can I check my order status?

You can check the status of your order by clicking "Track Order" below.

How can I return an item?

Click on "Return Button" in your account and select the reason to initiate return of your item. Our courier partner will pick it up from your place. Do keep the product tags and packaging intact. You can return an item within 3 days of delivery. ''Click here'' to read our Easy Return Policy.

Is it necessary to have an account to place an order on Homoeomeds?

Yes, it is necessarily have an account with us to place an order. It is highly recommended you to create a Homoeomeds account. This will allow you to have a track record with us regarding all your orders.

Can I leave items in my cart for future purchase?

Yes, you can leave items in your cart for future purchase provided you should be logged in while adding products in your cart and the same device is used for the purchase.

How do I know my order has been confirmed?

Once your order is placed and confirmed, you will see a confirmation page displaying a unique Order ID, shipping address with the estimated date of delivery. In addition, you will receive an SMS & email confirming your order details.

Can I order a product that is 'Out of Stock'?

'Out of Stock' means the products are not available with us and when your see the product is 'Out of Stock' you will have to do is add that product in to your Wishist, so that the notification will reach us and we see that your request of the product should be notify and fullfilled by us.

How can I cancel my order?

Click on "Cancel Order" in My Account. Choose the order, select the reason and submit!. You can cancel an order before delivery initiated.

When can I expect refund for my returned item?

Once we receive the original item, we will do quality check within 2 days and initiate refund.
- It will be initiated immediately to card/bank account. Your bank will take a maximum of 3 to 4 business days to credit this amount. We will also keep you updated on email and SMS.

I don't remember my password. Help!

Follow these steps to reset your password:

  1. Click on Login & Signup button
  2. Click on 'Forgot password?'
  3. Enter your registered e-mail address or mobile number and we will send you a security code to reset your password
  4. Enter the code and set a new password
  5. Save the changes
